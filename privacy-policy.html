Privacy Policy

privacy policy

What information do we gather?

We only gather personally identifiable information when you sign into our site or provide information in our form fields. You might be requested to provide your full name, email address, mailing address and phone number.

What do we do with this information?

The details that we gather might be used in following ways:

To personalize your experience:

Your information allows us to respond better to your individual needs.

To make our website better:

We constantly try to enhance our website performance and services based on the details and response we get from you.

To enhance our customer service:

Your details allow us to immediately respond to your customer service requests and support needs.

To send promotional emails:

The email address you give might be utilized to send promotional emails and special offers from time to time.

To get in touch with you:

We can contact you on your request on your provided details.

How do we safeguard your information?

We have taken different steps to maintain the security of your personal details when you provide, enter or access your personal information. Your details are stored in a password encrypted database.

Do we utilize Cookies?

Yes. Cookies are tiny files that a website transfers to its visitor’s hard drive via your web browser (If allowed) that allow the sites to identify your browser and record certain information. We utilize cookies to help us remember, understand and store your preferences for future and put together the site traffic and site interaction so we can enhance site experiences in coming future. Such information is recorded in an anonymous form that does not carry any of your personally identifiable information. We don’t collect any personally identifiable information unless it’s submitted directly via the fill-in form.

Remarketing

The cookies can be used to show remarketing advertisements depending upon the user’s old visits to our website across the internet. Remarketing advertisements are shown through Google’s display-advertisement network. Visitors can stop the Google’s use of cookies anytime by accessing Google Ads Preference Manager.

Do we disclose these details to outside parties?

We do not distribute, sell, trade or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. That does not include the third party entities that assist us in managing our website, operating our business or serving you, so as long as these parties consent to keeping the details confidential. We might disclose the details to comply with the law, enforce our site policies or safeguard our’s or other’s rights, property and safety. The non-personally identifiable information however can be shared with different parties for advertising and other purposes.

Third party links

Sometimes, we might include or allow third party products or services on our site. Such sites have different privacy policies. We will not be held responsible for the content or activities of these third party sites.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This privacy policy is only applicable to details gathered through website and not the details gathered offline.
